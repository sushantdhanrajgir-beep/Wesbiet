Our Story

A Question That Became a Place

[Resort Name] began as a question: what would happen if a property were made not for guests, but for human beings? If hospitality meant creating space to breathe, rather than filling every moment with activity?

The answer took years to build. It fills several acres of [region] and holds twelve suites, each shaped by an artist who believes that beauty is not decorative — it is structural.

Every path on the property was walked before it was paved. Every window was positioned before its wall was built. The result is a place where the line between architecture and nature is not blurred — it is deliberately, lovingly erased.

Hotel · FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you arrive — and a few things you may not have thought to ask.

General

Your Questions, Answered

How many suites does [Resort Name] have?+
We have twelve suites across three collections: the Signature Collection (Forest View), the Lakeside Collection, and the Artist's Collection. Every suite includes a private plunge pool, bespoke artwork, and in-suite dining.
Is there a minimum stay requirement?+
We recommend a minimum of two nights to fully experience the property, though there is no strict policy. Wellness programmes and most curated experiences are designed around stays of three nights or more.
Do you offer a digital detox policy?+
Wi-Fi is available on request, but we gently encourage guests to consider a digital detox during their stay. Many guests tell us it is the best decision of the trip. We offer a dedicated digital detox programme as part of our wellness package.
Is the property available for exclusive buyout?+
Yes — full property buyout is available for weddings, corporate retreats, and family gatherings. This gives your group complete privacy across all twelve suites and all common spaces. Please contact our events team for pricing and availability.
Are children welcome at [Resort Name]?+
We welcome families. We ask that children aged 8 and above stay in the suites, and we offer curated nature and art experiences designed for younger guests. Please inform us at the time of booking so we can prepare appropriately.
What dining options are available?+
We offer alfresco garden dining, private poolside dinners, rooftop dining (where views permit), and 24/7 in-suite dining. Our menu changes daily based on the kitchen garden harvest and is available in vegetarian, vegan, and Jain versions.
How do I make a reservation?+
You can book directly through our website, by phone, or via WhatsApp. We strongly recommend direct booking — it guarantees the best rates, flexibility, and direct communication with our team before and during your stay.
What is your cancellation policy?+
We offer full cancellation up to 14 days before check-in. Cancellations within 14 days are subject to a one-night charge. Event and wedding bookings have separate terms — please refer to your event contract or contact our reservations team.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to answer any enquiry by phone, email, or WhatsApp.

Our Policies

Booking & Stay Terms

Reservation Policy
A 50% advance payment is required to confirm a reservation. The remaining balance is due 14 days before check-in. All rates are per suite per night and inclusive of taxes unless otherwise stated.
Cancellation Policy
Full cancellation with refund is available up to 14 days before check-in. Cancellations within 14 days of arrival are subject to a one-night charge. No-shows are charged in full.
Check-In & Check-Out
Check-in from 2:00 PM. Check-out by 12:00 PM noon. Early check-in and late check-out are available on request, subject to availability, at an additional charge.
Children & Pets
Guests aged 8 and above are welcome. Pets are not permitted on the property. Please contact us before booking to discuss special requirements for younger guests.
Privacy Policy
We collect only the information necessary to complete your reservation and improve your experience. We do not share or sell guest data to third parties.
